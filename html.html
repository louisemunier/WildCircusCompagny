SHORT DESCRIPTION

The Wild Circus Compagny was fonded in 1886 in the little town of Briniti (South France) by two brothers. They thought that there were not enough animations in their city. They had a passion for trip and circus and their dream is to travel all over the world. That how they import at Britini the circus Art at the age of twenty and twenty free. The little circus begin with three or four acts, then a decade and more. Their project was a real success among the population. The compagny grew little by little as their popularity. In each city where the compagny perform, a competition to make local talents known is organized a few days before. The winners have the opportunity to do a performance during a Wild Circus Compagny show. A great communication!

Today, their children and their great-grandchildren take over with the same passion. We could see acrobats, tightropes walking, modern jugglers, magicians, etc. in sumptuous decors and costums. Performances are regulary change for the pleasure of kids' and adults' eyes.

Elephant Training

Animals are always be in the Wild Circus Compagny and keep performing with their wonderful trainers. Elephants, bears, lions, sea lions, cats, tigers, all are used and treat in complete safty and good conditions for the animals.

You can of course see the menagerie after the show.

Juggler of fire

During this performance jugglers play with fire and the technologies. Spectactors are throw in the obscurity to admirate the numerous effects with music

This is a original and modern show which will marvel you.

Street circus

The novelty of the compagny is to go meet the population directly in the street. Generally, this meeting take place after the competition of the local talents.

Street performances are less sophisticated that performances in classic Wils Circus Compagny, but they are more funny and interactive. Furthermore, th show is free.
